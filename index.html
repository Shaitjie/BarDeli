About BarDeli

Founded in 2019, BarDeli is dedicated to providing exceptional catering services that bring your events to life. We specialize in corporate functions but cater for weddings, funerals, and private parties. We offer full service including furniture, décor, flowers, mobile bars, staffing, tents, and sound equipment — everything you need for an unforgettable event.
